# Philosophy

## Purpose

This document defines the principles that guide my decisions as a professional, learner, and person.

These principles are intended to remain stable over time. While technologies, tools, and career opportunities may
change, the ideas described here should continue to guide how I work and live.

---

## Continuous Learning

Learning is a permanent way of life, not a temporary phase.

Every day should include time dedicated to improving my knowledge, whether through reading, building projects, teaching,
or experimenting.

Curiosity is a habit that should be maintained throughout my career.

---

## Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence is a tool that amplifies my capabilities, not a replacement for my judgment.

I use AI to accelerate learning, explore ideas, review code, and improve productivity.

However, I never outsource understanding.

Any code I write or accept should be understandable, explainable, and maintainable.

---

## Technical Excellence

I value quality over speed whenever possible.

I strive to produce software that is:

- Correct
- Readable
- Maintainable
- Well documented
- Easy to evolve

Technical excellence is achieved through continuous improvement rather than perfection.

---

## Career

I intentionally pursue technical mastery rather than management responsibilities.

My goal is to become a highly capable Unreal Engine engineer who can solve difficult problems, build high-quality
software, and contribute to the success of a team through technical expertise.

Titles are less important than the ability to create value.

---

## Collaboration

Software is built by teams.

I value professionalism, clear communication, respect, and knowledge sharing.

Helping others grow is part of being a good engineer.

---

## Life Balance

Professional success should never come at the expense of family, faith, health, or personal well-being.

A sustainable career is more valuable than temporary periods of excessive productivity.

Balance is not a reward for success; it is part of success.

---

## Recreation

Rest and recreation are essential parts of a healthy life.

Enjoying video games, spending time with family, and disconnecting from work are not distractions from productivity—they
help sustain it over the long term.

---

## Faith

My Christian faith is one of the foundations of my life.

Professional decisions should remain compatible with the values and priorities that arise from that faith.

Career success should never replace spiritual growth.

---

## Long-Term Perspective

I aim to build a career that can be sustained for decades.

This means continuously adapting to new technologies, embracing change without fear, and using new tools responsibly
while preserving the ability to think critically and solve problems independently.
