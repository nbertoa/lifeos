# Profile

## Purpose

This is the canonical concise profile of Nicolás Bertoa. It gives people and AI assistants enough identity context to interpret LifeOS without turning the profile into a résumé or full biography.

## Identity

Nicolás Bertoa is a software engineer focused on hands-on technical work, maintainable software architecture, modern C++, and Unreal Engine. He values correctness, clear responsibility, simplicity, continuous learning, and sharing knowledge.

His professional direction favors technical mastery over management. His long-term goal is to deepen his Unreal Engine expertise while remaining an engineer who can solve difficult problems and deliver practical value.

## Professional Focus

Nicolás's principal technical areas are:

- Unreal Engine 4 and 5.
- Modern C++ and C++/Blueprint system boundaries.
- Software and gameplay architecture.
- Computer graphics, OpenGL, and GLSL.
- 3D mathematics.
- Performance and memory optimization.
- Debugging, prototyping, and interactive R&D.

His career includes Intel Corporation and employment at Globant, with long-term client work for DreamWorks Animation and Sony Interactive Entertainment R&D. The [Professional Journey](../Career/ProfessionalJourney.md) is the canonical record of roles, dates, relationships, and responsibilities.

He is recorded as an Unreal Authorized Instructor at the Silver level; current public use of that credential should be checked against the canonical career record.

## Working Identity

Nicolás prefers:

- Technical contribution and architecture over people-management roles.
- Clear, maintainable systems over clever or speculative designs.
