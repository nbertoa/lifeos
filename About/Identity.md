# Identity

## Mission

Nicolás Bertoa is a software engineer committed to building high-quality software, continuously expanding his technical
expertise, and sharing knowledge with others.

His long-term professional goal is to achieve technical mastery in Unreal Engine while remaining a hands-on engineer
focused on delivering practical value through software.

## Professional Identity

Nicolás intentionally pursues technical excellence over management roles.

He enjoys solving complex engineering problems, designing maintainable software architectures, and building high-quality
prototypes using modern C++ and Unreal Engine.

Artificial Intelligence is considered an integral part of his engineering workflow, acting as a collaborative partner
that enhances productivity, learning, and software quality rather than replacing engineering judgment.

## Long-Term Vision

Ten years from now, Nicolás hopes to be recognized as an engineer who:

- Delivers high-quality software.
- Can be trusted with complex technical challenges.
- Produces maintainable and understandable code.
- Learns continuously.
- Collaborates effectively with others.
- Shares knowledge openly.
- Uses AI responsibly as part of the software development process.

## Personal Philosophy

Professional excellence should never come at the expense of life balance.

Learning, family, faith, work, and personal well-being are complementary parts of a meaningful life rather than
competing priorities.

Continuous learning is not treated as a temporary goal but as a permanent way of living.
